FAQ

Pricing Questions

What's included in the inspection price?

All prices include tool deployment, data acquisition, processing, and reporting. Travel costs for remote locations may be additional.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. We'll prorate the difference and apply it to your next inspection.

Are there any setup fees?

No, there are no setup fees. You only pay for the inspections you schedule.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, clients scheduling 10+ inspections per year qualify for volume discounts. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.
