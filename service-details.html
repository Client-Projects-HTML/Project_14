Inspection Technologies

Multiple advanced technologies to detect and characterize all types of pipeline anomalies.

Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL)

High-resolution metal loss detection for general corrosion and pitting. Our MFL tools provide accurate wall thickness measurements and defect sizing with 90%+ accuracy.

Ultrasonic Testing (UT)

High-accuracy wall thickness measurement and crack detection. UT technology provides precise metal loss quantification and is ideal for crack-like defect detection.

Electromagnetic Acoustic (EMAT)

Advanced crack detection and stress corrosion cracking assessment. EMAT technology excels at detecting axially oriented cracks and crack colonies.
