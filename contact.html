FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to emergency inspection requests?

We maintain 24/7 emergency response capabilities and can typically mobilize inspection crews within 24-48 hours for critical situations.

What types of pipelines do you inspect?

We inspect all types of pipelines including oil, gas, refined products, water, and chemical pipelines. Our tools accommodate diameters from 4" to 56".

How long does it take to receive inspection reports?

Preliminary results are typically available within 48 hours. Comprehensive reports with full analysis are delivered within 2-4 weeks depending on project scope.

Do you offer international services?

Yes, we operate globally with projects completed across North America, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East. We comply with local regulations in all operating regions.
