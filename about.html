Our Story

Built on Excellence and Innovation

Founded in 2011, PipelinePro began with a simple mission: to provide the most accurate, reliable, and comprehensive pipeline inspection services in the industry. What started as a small team of dedicated engineers has grown into a global leader in pipeline integrity management.

Our journey has been defined by continuous innovation. We were among the first to adopt advanced MFL and UT inspection technologies, and we've consistently invested in research and development to stay at the forefront of the industry.

Today, we serve major energy companies across six continents, providing critical inspection services that help ensure the safety and reliability of the world's pipeline infrastructure.

Our Mission

Protecting Infrastructure, Ensuring Safety

To provide world-class pipeline inspection and integrity management services that ensure the safe, reliable, and efficient operation of energy infrastructure worldwide. We are committed to leveraging cutting-edge technology and expert analysis to protect our clients' assets and the environment.

Our Vision

The Global Standard in Pipeline Integrity

To be the world's most trusted partner for pipeline inspection and integrity management, setting the industry standard for accuracy, reliability, and innovation.
